## Key Insights

### 1. "lättlästa böcker" is the strategic core keyword
590 searches/month, HIGH competition, CPC 0.79. This is the single most valuable keyword for Argasso:
- Specific enough that big retailers (Adlibris, Bokus) don't fully own it
- Directly describes Argasso's unique positioning
- Use as the primary keyword in H1s, page titles, and meta descriptions

### 2. "barnböcker" is a volume play, not a ranking play
3,600/month but dominated by large booksellers. Argasso should **include it** in page copy and titles to capture the long tail, but shouldn't expect to rank #1 for it alone.

### 3. "lättlästa böcker barn" (260/mo) is the sweet spot long-tail
Combining "lättlästa böcker" (Argasso's niche) + "barn" (primary audience) creates a 260/month keyword with meaningful volume and more attainable competition. This should be the primary target for the `/bocker/barnbocker` category page.

### 4. "lättläst text för barn" — GSC anomaly to investigate
Google Search Console shows this keyword ranking at position 3.14 with 641 impressions but **0 clicks**. This suggests Argasso appears in a featured snippet or Knowledge Panel element that users don't click. Investigate which page is triggering this impression and whether a dedicated page could capture clicks.

### 5. Graphic novels are a niche worth owning
"grafisk roman" + "grafiska romaner" each get 90/month with moderate competition. Argasso's `/bocker/ungdomsbocker/grafiska-romaner` page could own this term with a proper SEO title and description.

### 6. Brand awareness is tiny
Only 40 monthly searches for "Argasso". Growth comes from organic discovery, not brand recall — which makes ranking for category keywords critical.
